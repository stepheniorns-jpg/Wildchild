Account management

Delete your account

You can delete your WildChild Quest account and all associated data at any time, either directly in the app or by contacting us.

Choose a method

★ Recommended

Delete in the app

The fastest way — your account and all data are removed instantly.

  1. 1 Open WildChild Quest on your device
  2. 2 Go to Profile → Settings
  3. 3 Tap Account → Delete Account
  4. 4 Confirm the deletion — this takes effect immediately
Email request

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us and we'll delete your account within 7 days.

  1. 1 Send an email to the address below
  2. 2 Include the email address linked to your account
  3. 3 We'll confirm deletion within 7 days
apps@sjidigital.co.nz

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

Your profile and display name
All saved quests and progress
Account credentials
Activity and adventure history
Subscription records
Any uploaded photos or content
Device preferences and settings
All associated metadata

This action is permanent. Deleted accounts and data cannot be recovered. If you have an active subscription, cancel it in the App Store or Google Play before deleting your account to avoid future charges.
