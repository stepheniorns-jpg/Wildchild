Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Premium subscription?

If you've reinstalled the app or moved to a new device, open the WildChild Quest app, navigate to the Settings tab, and tap the Restore purchases button under the Premium section.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

WildChild Quest subscriptions are managed securely by Apple. To cancel or change your plan:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap your name at the top of the screen.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select WildChild Quest and choose your preferred option.

How do I delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account and all associated family data from within the app. Go to the Settings tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete account. Please note this action cannot be undone.
