Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to WildChildQuest. We are committed to protecting your family’s privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create a family account, we collect your email address, password, and basic profile information.
  • Family & Child Profiles: To provide the core adventure experience, you may create profiles for your children within your account. We only collect the information you voluntarily provide (e.g., nicknames, avatars, and progress within the app).
  • Device & Usage Data: We collect standard diagnostic data, crash logs, and usage metrics to improve the app's performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your family's account and subscription.
  • To save your progress and customize the adventures.
  • To provide customer support and communicate updates.
  • To analyze app performance and fix technical issues.

Children's Privacy

WildChildQuest is designed to be used by families. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. All child profiles are managed through the parent's primary account. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child without parental consent, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using industry-standard authentication and database services. While we take strong measures to protect your information, no digital storage is 100% secure.
